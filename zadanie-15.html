Who are we?

Born in 2019, by the hands of 2 sisters and their mother, GUAJA. is the culmination of a life long dream. Every piece reflects the dedication and detali-driven artistry of its creators, who oversee every stage of production.

Born from humble beginnings at a kitchen table and crafted without external funding, GUAJA has been bulit step by step, with love and intention.

In its first year, every garment was designed, cut, and sewn by hand by the founders. Today, the business has grown whlie staying true to its roots, producing each piece exclusively with Portuguese manufacturers who uphold fair pay and prioritize exceptional quality.

Our commitment

Atelier handcrafter

Proudly rooted in family heritage, our hands-on approach ensures that every garment reflects our commitment to enduring quality and timeless style.

Elevated everyday wear

Elevated everyday wear, designed to move with you. Efortless tailoring, fluid sillhouettes, and considered details-made to fit the modern woman rhytm.

Handmade in Portugal

What began in the hands of our founders now lives in the skilled hands of local factories and artisans-craftspeople with decades of experience who bring each design to life with care and precision.

On a sustainable path

We give priority to organic, natural, recycled, certain types of cotton, linens, silks and even some viscose. However, you will also find polyesters in our collection, and in these cases, we opt for deadstock fabrics. Our packaging is made of recycled cardboard and it is plastic-free, as are all its components.
